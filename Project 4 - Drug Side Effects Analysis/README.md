# 💊 Drug Analysis: Side Effects & Clinical Insights

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing drug data to uncover patterns in **side effects, medical conditions, drug classes, and safety factors** using exploratory data analysis (EDA).

The goal is to combine **clinical knowledge (Pharm D)** with **data analytics** to generate meaningful healthcare insights.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze distribution of drugs across medical conditions
* Identify the most common side effects
* Study prescription patterns (Rx vs OTC)
* Evaluate drug effectiveness using ratings
* Assess safety factors such as:

  * Alcohol interaction
  * Pregnancy risk
  * Side effect severity

---

## 📊 Dataset Information

* Total Records: **~2900+ drugs**
* Features include:

  * Drug Name
  * Medical Condition
  * Side Effects
  * Drug Class
  * Rating
  * Number of Reviews
  * Rx/OTC
  * Pregnancy Category
  * Alcohol Interaction

---

## 🧹 Data Preprocessing

* Missing values handled appropriately
* Side effects cleaned and standardized
* Non-informative terms removed (e.g., lips, tongue)
* Ratings filtered to remove invalid values
* Feature engineering:

  * Side effect severity classification
  * Cleaned alcohol interaction categories

---

## 📈 Key Analyses Performed

### 🔹 Medical Condition Analysis

* Identified most commonly treated conditions
* Found high prevalence of symptomatic treatments

### 🔹 Side Effects Analysis

* Common side effects include:

  * Hives
  * Swelling
  * Breathing difficulty
* Indicates hypersensitivity reactions

### 🔹 Drug Class Analysis

* Dominant classes:

  * Respiratory drugs
  * Anti-inflammatory agents
  * Dermatological drugs

### 🔹 Rating Analysis

* Most drugs show moderate to high ratings
* Certain conditions have lower ratings → unmet clinical needs

### 🔹 Review Count vs Rating

* Higher review counts → more stable ratings
* Indicates reliability of widely used drugs

### 🔹 Safety Analysis

* Rx drugs dominate over OTC
* Many drugs fall under pregnancy categories **B and C**
* Severe side effects present → need monitoring
* Alcohol interaction data shows missing values (data limitation)

---

## 📊 Visualizations

Key visualizations include:

* Bar charts (conditions, drug classes)
* Pie charts (Rx vs OTC, severity)
* Histogram (rating distribution)
* Scatter plot (reviews vs rating)

---

## 🧠 Key Insights

* Drug usage reflects common clinical conditions
* Allergic reactions are a major safety concern
* Treatment effectiveness varies across conditions
* Safety considerations are critical in prescribing

---

## ⚠️ Limitations

* Missing data in alcohol interaction
* Ratings may vary due to subjective patient reviews
* Dataset does not include dosage or duration
